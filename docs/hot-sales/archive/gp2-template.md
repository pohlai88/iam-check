## Spot-Selling Program for Piglet Orders — Sales Team Only

Dear Sales Team,

We are launching a **spot-selling program for piglet orders from GP2**, applicable to the Sales Team only.

Any eligible piglet order that is **received and officially registered before 8:30 PM on 09 July 2026, Vietnam time**, Sales Personnel will enjoy an additional support of:

## **VND100,000 per piglet**

This support is a **standalone spot-selling support**. It will not be affected by, linked to, deducted from, or traded off against any other routine policy, pricing arrangement, trade-off, incentive, rebate, promotion, or existing program.

---

## 1. Program Objective

The purpose of this spot-selling program is to support the Sales Team in securing quick and confirmed piglet orders from GP2, based on available quantity reported by GP2.

This program is designed for **confirmed spot orders only** and is not intended to replace, revise, or create precedent for any existing routine sales policy or commercial arrangement.

---

## 2. Eligible Participants

This program is applicable to:

* Sales Team orders only.
* Orders submitted by authorized Sales Team members.
* Orders officially received and registered before the deadline.
* Orders linked to confirmed customer demand.

This program is **not open for general application outside the Sales Team** unless approved by management.

---

## 3. Ordering Deadline

All eligible orders must be submitted and officially registered before:

## **8:30 PM, 09 July 2026, Vietnam time**

Only orders recorded in the official spot-selling registration tracker before the cut-off time will be considered eligible.

Late orders, verbal orders, unregistered orders, or orders submitted after the deadline will not qualify for the VND100,000 per piglet support.

The program applies to piglets from GP2 under the following categories:

| July / T7      | 8kg, 10kg, 15kg |
| August / T8    | 8kg, 10kg, 15kg |
| September / T9 | 8kg, 10kg, 15kg |

Final allocation will depend on actual piglet availability from GP2 and the confirmed category available at the time of pickup.

---

## 5. Program Support

Eligible confirmed orders will receive:

## **VND100,000 support per piglet**

This support is:

* Standalone.
* Applicable only to eligible and registered spot orders.
* Not tied to any other program.
* Not affected by other routine policy, trade-off, rebate, discount, or incentive.
* Not used to offset, reduce, revise, or replace any existing commercial arrangement.
* Applicable only to the confirmed and completed quantity.

The VND100,000 per piglet support will only be applied after the order is confirmed and completed according to the program conditions.

## 6. Quantity Availability

The available quantity will be based strictly on the final quantity reported and confirmed by GP2.

The tentative quantity is approximately:

## **1,400 piglets per week**

However, this quantity is **not guaranteed**.

Based on the latest indication from Dr. Eric, the actual available quantity may be **lower**, but it will not be higher than the quantity confirmed by GP2.

Therefore:

* Registration does not guarantee full allocation.
* Final quantity is subject to GP2 confirmation.
* The company reserves the right to reduce, reject, or reallocate orders if the available quantity is lower than total demand.

---

## 7. Allocation Rule

Allocation will be managed based on:

## **First-Come-First-Serve + Customer Priority Order**

This means:

1. Orders must be officially received and registered before the deadline.
2. Priority customer orders may be considered first, based on the approved customer priority order.
3. Within the same priority level, allocation will follow the first-come-first-serve principle based on official registration time.
4. Final allocation remains subject to GP2’s confirmed available quantity.
5. In case total demand exceeds supply, the company reserves the right to adjust the confirmed quantity.

This rule is intended to ensure fairness, transparency, and better control of limited supply.

---

## 10. Transferability and Deposit Rule

Confirmed orders are **transferable**, subject to proper registration and approval.

However:

## **Deposit paid is non-refundable**

This means:

* The order may be transferred to another eligible customer or buyer.
* The transfer must be updated in the official registration tracker.
* The transfer must be approved by the authorized program owner or management.
* Any deposit already paid will not be refunded.
* Cancellation, no-show, or failure to pick up will not entitle the customer to a deposit refund.
* Deposit treatment in case of transfer must follow company approval and finance confirmation.

This rule is to prevent false booking, speculative booking, and last-minute cancellation.

## 11. Cancellation, No-Show, and Order Changes

Once an order is confirmed, any cancellation, reduction, transfer, or change must be approved and updated in the official tracker.

The following orders will not be eligible for the VND100,000 per piglet support:

* Cancelled orders.
* No-show pickup orders.
* Orders not picked up within the assigned schedule.
* Orders transferred without approval.
* Orders not recorded in the official tracker.
* Orders submitted after the deadline.
* Orders with unpaid or unclear deposit status, where deposit is required.

The company reserves the right to reallocate cancelled or uncollected quantity to other eligible customers.

---

## 12. Non-Combination and Standalone Policy

This spot-selling program is a standalone program.

It will not be:

* Combined with other promotions.
* Linked to routine pricing policy.
* Offset against any existing rebate or incentive.
* Used as a trade-off against other commercial terms.
* Treated as a revision of standard sales policy.
* Used as a precedent for future orders.

Any exception must be approved in writing by management.

## 13. Final Confirmation

The Sales Team must not confirm final quantity to customers until the allocation has been officially confirmed.

The final confirmed order will depend on:

* GP2 final available quantity.
* Customer priority order.
* Official registration time.
* Piglet category availability.
* Deposit status, where applicable.

Only confirmed and completed orders will enjoy the VND100,000 per piglet support.
